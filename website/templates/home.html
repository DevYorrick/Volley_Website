Volleyball organized by the HAN

Every Monday, Wednesday and Friday we play volleyball together with students from the HAN and some other universities.
Joining volleyball is completely free! For more information feel free to contact the Coordinator or send a message in the whatsapp group chat.
Press sign up to get started, after making an account hover over attendance list and select sign in, here you will be able to select which day you would like to join.
People with all skill levels are welcome, we will just be playing some games with everyone and the focus is on having fun! Everyone can learn how to play.
